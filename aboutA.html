Who i am

I am a young girl from the Democratic, Republic of Congo, born on October 29, 2008 in Goma (Carmel Hospital) and who always seeks success in life and believes in the power of education. I started my educational journey when I was only three years old. Many people doubted whether I could succeed because I had not attended infant school, but I was determined to prove them wrong.

In short, this is my story — a story of determination, curiosity, and faith. I believe in using education, technology, and leadership to build a better future for myself and for the people of the Democratic Republic of Congo.
